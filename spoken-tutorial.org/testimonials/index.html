Mehala Nagarajah, Program Manager - VIDYA Integrated Development for Youth and Adults

Mehala Nagarajah, Program Manager - VIDYA Integrated Development for Youth and Adults

We, VIDYA Computer Learning & Resource Center (CLRC), have partnered with Spoken Tutorial since 2 years now. Most of the VIDYA's trainers have learnt GIMP & Inkscape. Inkscape is extremely useful to us and we have become very proficient in poster-making and creating colourful awareness & class decor posters.

CLRC students find the industry recognized certificate very useful. Our school partnership school teachers also get trained in creating posters using Inkscape during summer vacation. This year, we integrated GIMP & Inkscape in our "Beyond School Computer Curriculum" for 6th & 7th std respectively.

Thank you Prof. Kannan & Nancy for introducing us to such a wonderful self-learning platform. We are still exploring & will most likely use the platform to its fullest potential for VIDYA's beneficiaries.
